# Support

Need help with Next Coach? We're here for you.

## Reporting a Bug

If something isn't working as expected, please [open a bug report](https://github.com/gariem/next-coach-resources/issues/new?template=bug_report.md) on GitHub. Include your device model, iOS version, and steps to reproduce the issue.

## Requesting a Feature

Have an idea to make Next Coach better? [Submit a feature request](https://github.com/gariem/next-coach-resources/issues/new?template=feature_request.md) and tell us what you'd like to see.

## Suggest a Cue Game

Have a song that would make a great cue game? [Submit a cue game request](https://github.com/gariem/next-coach-resources/issues/new?template=cue_game_request.md) — just tell us the song, which section to use, and what exercise should go with it. We'll handle the rest.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Do I need an Apple Music subscription?
Yes. Next Coach uses Apple Music to play tracks during cue games. An active Apple Music subscription is required for music playback.

### Why does the app ask for Apple Music permission?
Next Coach needs access to Apple Music to play songs that are synced with coaching cues. The app does not access your library or listening history — it only plays pre-configured tracks for each game.

### Does the app work on iPad?
Yes. Next Coach supports both iPhone and iPad with layouts optimized for each device.

### Is my data backed up to iCloud?
Session plans and preferences are stored locally on your device. They are not synced to iCloud or any cloud service.

### Is the app free?
Yes. Next Coach is completely free with no in-app purchases or ads.

## Contact

For any other questions, [open an issue on GitHub](https://github.com/gariem/next-coach-resources/issues).
