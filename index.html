Next Coach is a coaching assistant for martial arts instructors. Run music-synced cue games, plan your sessions, and time your intervals — all from your iPhone or iPad.

Download on the App Store

Three tools, one app

Everything you need to run a great training session.

♫

Cue Games

Play Apple Music tracks with coaching cues synced to the beat. Visual glow effects and haptic feedback keep you in the flow — so you can focus on your athletes, not a stopwatch.

☰

Session Planner

Structure your training with timed, color-coded segments. Set a weekly schedule, track your progress through each block, and keep every class on time.

⏱

Training Timer

Configurable work/rest intervals with automatic whistle triggers. Choose from multiple whistle sounds and customize colors to match your coaching style.
