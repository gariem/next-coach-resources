# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** April 3, 2026

Emilio Rios ("we", "us", "our") built **Next Coach** as a free app. This page describes how the app handles information.

## Information We Collect

Next Coach is designed to operate locally on your device. **We do not directly collect, transmit, or have access to any personal data.**

### Data stored on your device

- **Whistle preferences** — Your chosen whistle sound, color theme, icon style, and volume are saved using standard iOS preferences (UserDefaults). We do not access this data remotely.
- **Session plans** — Any session plans you create are saved as a local file in the app's documents directory. We do not access this data remotely.

Note: Data stored on your device may be included in iOS backups (including iCloud backups) based on your device settings. This is managed by iOS, not by us.

### Apple Music

Next Coach uses Apple's **MusicKit** framework to play music during cue games. When you grant Apple Music access:

- The app requests permission to play media through Apple Music on your device.
- Track metadata (song title, artist, artwork) is fetched from Apple's servers through MusicKit.
- The app plays specific tracks that are pre-configured for each cue game. It does not browse or read your personal music library, playlists, or listening history.
- All communication with Apple Music services is handled by Apple's MusicKit framework. Apple's own [privacy policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/) governs how Apple processes that data.

## Data We Do NOT Directly Collect

To the best of our knowledge, the app does not:

- Collect analytics or usage data
- Use crash reporting services
- Use advertising or ad identifiers
- Require account creation or login
- Access location, contacts, photos, camera, or microphone
- Share data with third parties

## Third-Party Services

The only external service used by the app is **Apple Music**, accessed through Apple's MusicKit framework. We do not knowingly integrate other third-party services that collect user data.

## Children's Privacy

Next Coach does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.
